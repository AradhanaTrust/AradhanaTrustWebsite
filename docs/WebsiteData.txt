Aradhana Dharmika Trust is a Hindu charitable and religious trust dedicated to dharma, seva and samskara. From Kodihalli Village in Malur Taluk, we organize satsangs, spiritual discourses, value-based programs and annadana to support the community and preserve our spiritual heritage.


Promotion of Dharma
To promote the values and teachings of Sanatana Dharma through satsangs, pravachanas, spiritual study circles and dharmic publications.

Education & Sanskara
To encourage value-based education, cultural learning and the transmission of spiritual knowledge to younger generations.

Annadana & Seva
To organize annadana (food distribution), support for the needy, and other charitable activities serving society without discrimination.

Preservation of Culture
To preserve and promote Indian cultural heritage, traditional arts, music, and spiritual practices for future generations.

About Aradhana Dharmika Trust
Aradhana Dharmika Trust is a Hindu charitable and religious trust established to preserve and promote Sanatana Dharma and Indian cultural values. The trust organizes satsangs, spiritual discourses, value-based camps, cultural programs and service activities such as annadana and support for the needy. The trust functions with transparency and accountability, guided by its board of trustees and in accordance with the laws applicable to charitable and religious trusts in India.
